Operation Wildwood · Dossier

In 1953, something woke up in the forest.

The atomic tests were classified. What crawled out of them wasn't.

The Signal Corps was formed in secret to contain it. The Biosynth Collective spun out of the Atomic Energy Commission with orders to engineer a weapon that could think like its prey. They succeeded — twice. The second one is still missing.

What you hold is a recovered field dossier: seventy-eight contact reports cataloguing the factions, the monsters, and the shape of a war the government still denies. Every card is a briefing. Every briefing is a warning.

— transcribed from the back cover, Operation Wildwood Field Dossier, 1953 reissue

Frequently Asked

Intel requests, answered.

When does the deck ship?

Field Decks begin shipping December 2026. Collector's, Field Agent, and Patron tiers (which require additional custom production) ship Q1 2027.

Where are you printing?

USA-based card printer with experience on premium collector decks. Final printer selected after the campaign closes based on final order volume.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — worldwide. International shipping is calculated in the pledge manager after the campaign closes based on destination.

Can I add merch without a deck?

Yes. The Transmission tier ($15) is digital-only, and you can add any merch item through the pledge manager. There will also be a stand-alone merch cart opened alongside fulfillment.

Is the game real?

The Sasquatch Wars game is in active pre-production. A funded campaign at $150k unlocks the greenlit vertical slice; $250k funds the full first chapter. Every physical backer gets access.

What happens if you don't fund?

All pledges are refunded. No charges until the campaign succeeds. We will re-launch with a smaller scope.

Who owns the art?

All artwork, writing, and design is © Gina Cox, independent creator at ginalyncox.com. Backers receive the deck and merch as licensed consumer goods — no commercial reuse rights.

Is this AI art?

The art is directed, composed, and finished by Gina using a locked style bible. Generative tools are part of the painting process but every card is reviewed, corrected, and signed off by hand. The style bible, card meanings, lore, and design are 100% original IP.
